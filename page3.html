I was lucky enough to spend 3 days visiting Paris this month! I went with a friend and we were able to see many things while we were there.

Day 1

On our first, we started by visiting Montmartre right away. Our seconf stop was the Eiffel Tower (Check out that beautiful picture!) We ate at a cute bistro overlooking la Seine.

Day 2

ON our second day, we went shopping on Avenue des Champs Elysees. Every single person we saw was in the highest of fashion.

After we finished shopping, we visited some really beautiful places! We went to Notre Dame. We went on a macaron tour of Paris and ate all of the best macarons the city had to offer. After we finished shopping, we visited le louvre.

Day 3

The picture below is of me visiting the Arc de Troimphe. It's Huge! We also went to le Musee D'orsary. Seeing the work of Renoir, Monet, Van Gogh in person was competely breathtaking. I was surprised to see that the museum also had many sculptures including those bby Auguste Rodin, Paul Gauguin, and alfred Barye.
